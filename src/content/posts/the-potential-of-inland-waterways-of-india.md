<h2>What is Inland waterways?</h2>



<p><em>Inland waterway</em> is a network in the form of rivers, canals, backwaters and creeks that can be used for transportation in place of or in addition to roads and rails.</p>



<h2>Why Inland Waterways are Important?</h2>



<p>Inland Waterways are the cheapest means of transport and are most suitable for carrying low cost heavy and bulky materials to long distances.</p>



<p>The cost of&nbsp;<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Water-transport">water transport</a>&nbsp;in India is roughly 50&nbsp;paise&nbsp;a kilometre, as compared to&nbsp;₹1 by&nbsp;railways&nbsp;and&nbsp;₹1.5 by roads. Water transport is receiving significant attention in recent times&nbsp;as logistics costs in India are some of the highest among major countries.</p>



<p><strong>"India&nbsp;has an extensive network of&nbsp;inland waterways&nbsp;in the form of&nbsp;rivers,&nbsp;canals,&nbsp;backwaters&nbsp;and&nbsp;creeks. The total navigable length is 14,500&nbsp;km (9,000&nbsp;mi), out of which about 5,200&nbsp;km (3,200&nbsp;mi) of the river and 4,000&nbsp;km (2,500&nbsp;mi) of canals"&nbsp;-&nbsp;</strong><em>According&nbsp;to&nbsp;Wikipedia</em></p>



<p>Most of the inland waterways of India is unutilized.&nbsp;</p>



<p>According to a report, The total cargo moved (in tonne-kilometres) by inland waterways was 0.1% of the total inland traffic in India, compared to the 21% figure for the United States.</p>



<p>In order to increases waterways in inland transport,&nbsp;Modi government&nbsp;got the&nbsp;<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Waterways_Act,_2016">National Waterways Act, 2016</a>&nbsp;passed which proposed 106 additional National Waterways.&nbsp;Inland waterways in India consist of the&nbsp;Ganges-Bhagirathi-Hooghly&nbsp;rivers, the&nbsp;Brahmaputra, the Barak river, the rivers in Goa, the backwaters in Kerala, inland waters in&nbsp;Mumbai&nbsp;and the deltaic regions of the&nbsp;Godavari-Krishna&nbsp;rivers.</p>

<p>The Government is working to develop inland waterways as an alternative mode of transport in the country, which is cleaner and cheaper than both road and rail transport. There are 111 National Waterways in the country today.</p>



<h3>What Are The Advantages of Inland waterways?</h3>



<ul><li>Least transport cost for heavy and bulky cargos.</li><li>Capability&nbsp;of transporting&nbsp;large&nbsp;amount of cargos in one time from one destination to another.</li><li>A fuel efficient and&nbsp;environment&nbsp;friendly&nbsp;mode of transportation.</li><li>No need of huge investment for the infrastructure like other modes of transport and&nbsp;less&nbsp;annual cost for maintenance of river channels.</li><li>The cheapest means of transporting raw materials and finished goods of various industries along the river banks.</li><li>Increase in the employment in the marine se<a href="https://theprayas.com/merchant-navy/six-indian-sailors-dead-six-missing-after-2-ships-catch-fire-in-kerch-strait/">ctors</a></li></ul>
