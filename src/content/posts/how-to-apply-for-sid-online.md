<p>Many students don't apply for SID before and wait for the company to ask for SID and then they face issues regarding appointments.</p>



<p>A Seafarer's Identity Document (SID) is a document that verifies the identity and qualifications of a seafarer. It is an official document that is recognized by international maritime organizations and many countries around the world.</p>



<p>The SID contains information about the seafarer's personal details, such as their name, date of birth, and nationality, as well as their qualifications, such as their certifications and training courses completed. The document also includes a photograph and a unique identification number to ensure the seafarer's identity can be verified.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">DG Shipping SID card Apply</h2>



<p>The purpose of the SID is to ensure that seafarers are qualified and trained to work in their respective roles on board ships and to provide a standard form of identification for seafarers that are recognized internationally. It is required for seafarers to obtain a SID in order to work on most ships, including commercial vessels and passenger ships.</p>



<p>The requirements for obtaining a SID vary depending on the country of issue but generally involve completing certain training courses and meeting specific qualifications and experience requirements. The SID is typically valid for a set period of time, after which it must be renewed.</p>



<p>So, you should apply for SID even before the company asks for it.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Steps to Apply for SID online:</h2>



<p>Before applying for SID, you must check your DGS Profile details data are in order and correct, and if any corrections please correct them before applying.</p>



<p>You should use the Mozilla Firefox browser to apply for SID.</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Log into&nbsp;<a href="http://dgshippingbsid.in/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">http://dgshippingbsid.in</a>&nbsp;using your user id and password (It’s the same as what you use for logging into your profile)<br>The user id is Indos No.</li>



<li>Before proceeding you must read the general instructions and collect the required documents.</li>



<li>Click on the Link “Apply for SID”</li>



<li>Check the personal details in the form and click to continue. (Most of the data comes automatically and u need to verify the same</li>



<li><em>If data does not come automatically then first update your DG profile</em></li>



<li>Fill in the address details and click continue</li>



<li>Verify all the details before Submittingting the application. when all are in order submit the application.</li>



<li>After that Pay the fees online (300 INR). Avoid Visa debit card(sometimes there are some issues with the payment gateway)</li>



<li>After paying the fees, u need to wait for 1-2 working days for the payment to reflect on the DG website. (Don’t pay the fees again).</li>



<li>After payment gets reflected, then click on take the appointment, and there u will see all the slots available for all MMDs.</li>



<li>Click on “Schedule Appointment” on the screen to schedule an appointment.</li>



<li>Select the collection center along with the date and time from the slots available.</li>



<li>Your application would be transmitted online to the verification officer, who will check the application.</li>



<li>Once u book the appointment, take the printout of the acknowledgment and also take a printout of the application letter.</li>



<li>Within 10 days of submission of the application, you will receive a notification on the verification result of the application through an e-mail and SMS.</li>



<li>If you wish to reschedule your confirmed appointment (after payment of the fee), the same can be done do so for a maximum of two more occasions (excluding the first appointment). If you reschedule your appointment for more than two occasions, then you will have to pay the fee again. In other words, the fee once paid is valid only for three appointments.</li>



<li>Visit the MMD at the scheduled appointment day at least 10-15mins earlier with the application form, the acknowledgment, the Original Passport, and CDC. Data capturing personnel, after examining your documents would return the same and capture your biometric data i.e You will have to sign on a digital pad and after that, they will take your photograph with their own camera and that’s it. Job done.</li>



<li>Check the website and it will show that the documents are verified and sent for SID generation. Seafarer's Identity Document would be issued and printed.</li>



<li>The same shall be dispatched by speed post within a month.</li>



<li></li>
</ol>
