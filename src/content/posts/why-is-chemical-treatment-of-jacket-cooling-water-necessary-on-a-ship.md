<p>Jacket cooling water is used on ship to maintain the temperature of the main engine and  Chemical treatment of Jacket Cooling water in necessary for the following reasons: </p>



<ol>
<li>Corrosion prevention: chemical treatment with inhibitors helps to prevent the corrosion of the metal component in the cooling system, which can lead to costly damage and leaks. </li>



<li>Sludge formation control: Treatment chemicals prevent the formation of sludge and deposit in the system which can reduce heat transfer efficiency and clog the system. </li>



<li>Scale deposit prevention: proper chemical treatment prevents the scale formation that can form on the heating exchanging surfaces and reduces the efficiency and heat transfer. </li>



<li>pH control: Chemical treatment helps maintain the pH of the cooling water within a specific range, preventing acidic or alkaline conditions that can contribute to corrosion and scaling. </li>
</ol>
