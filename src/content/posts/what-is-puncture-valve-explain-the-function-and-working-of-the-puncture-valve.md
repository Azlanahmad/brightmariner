<p>A Puncture valve is used to stop the Fuel Injection. It is a device to positively stops the engine irrespective of the rack position. </p>



<p>Engine stops and shutdowns are carried out using the puncture valve.</p>



<p>It is fitted on the top part of the Fuel Pump.</p>



<p>A puncture valve in a fuel pump is a type of relief valve that helps to protect the fuel pump and other engine components from damage caused by excessive pressure. The fuel pump in a diesel engine is responsible for delivering fuel from the fuel tank to the injectors, which then deliver fuel to the combustion chamber.</p>



<p>Under normal operating conditions, the fuel pressure in the system is regulated by a pressure regulator. However, in the event of a malfunction, such as a clogged fuel filter or a malfunctioning pressure regulator, the fuel pressure can increase beyond safe levels. This can cause damage to the fuel pump, injectors, and other components, as well as potentially creating a safety hazard.</p>



<p>The puncture valve is designed to prevent this by opening and releasing excess pressure when the pressure in the fuel system exceeds a certain threshold. This helps to protect the fuel pump and other components from damage, as well as ensuring that the engine operates safely and efficiently.</p>



<p>In summary, a puncture valve in a fuel pump is a type of relief valve that helps to regulate fuel pressure and prevent damage to the fuel pump and other engine components.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Function and Working of Puncture Valve:</strong></h3>



<p>A Puncture valve is operated with starting air which is sent during Stop, Emergency Stop or in event of actuation of the shutdown&nbsp;system of the main engine. </p>



<p>It reduces the high pressure of the fuel oil by connecting the high-pressure side to the pump body, thereby stopping the injection of the fuel. </p>

<p><strong>The puncture valve</strong>&nbsp;is operated in the event of actuation of the shutdown&nbsp;<strong>system</strong>, during the air start sequence or when excessive leakage is detected from the double skinned&nbsp;<strong>fuel</strong>&nbsp;pipes.&nbsp;</p>



<p><strong>Puncture valve</strong>&nbsp;actuation returns the&nbsp;<strong>fuel</strong>&nbsp;oil to&nbsp;<strong>fuel pump</strong>&nbsp;housing and no injection takes place.</p>



<p>If allows fuel recirculation when the engine is stopped since oil pressure is not totally bypassed.</p>



<p>It is operated by pneumatic air pressure.</p>



<p>It is used in MAN B&amp;W engine.</p>
