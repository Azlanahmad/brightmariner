<h1 class="wp-block-heading" id="synergy-exam-2022-syllabus">Synergy Exam 2025 syllabus:</h1>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="for-engine-cadets-2022">For Engine Cadets 2025:</h2>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>The examination will be in the online/offline format of duration 3 hours (180 minutes).</li>



<li>The examination will be of the objective type comprising 200 questions. A total of 100 questions will test knowledge and 100 questions that test aptitude.</li>



<li>Each question answered correctly is awarded 1 mark and every question answered incorrectly is awarded 0.25 negative mark. Un-attempted questions are not marked (no plus or minus marks).</li>
</ul>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Testing Areas for knowledge: (100 questions)</strong></h3>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Marine Engines.</li>



<li>All Marine Auxiliary machinery.</li>



<li>Machine design and drawing</li>



<li> Basic Ship Construction and Naval architecture.</li>



<li>Fire Safety- Knowledge of basic firefighting equipment</li>



<li>Materials and metallurgy</li>



<li>Basic Electricity and Electronics.</li>



<li>Basic Physics and Chemistry.</li>



<li>Other questions in the knowledge area will be from the approved syllabus of BE (Marine Eng.) / B.E (Mech.), as the case may be.</li>
</ul>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Testing areas for Aptitude: (100 questions)</h3>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Numerical Reasoning and Data Interpretation. (25 questions)</li>



<li>Mechanical reasoning. (20 questions)</li>



<li>Spatial and diagrammatic reasoning. (30 questions)</li>



<li>Verbal Comprehension. (25 questions)</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="synergy-exam-2022-syallabus-for-deck-cadet">Synergy Exam 2025 syllabus for Deck Cadet:</h2>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>The examination will be of the online format of duration 3 hours (180 minutes). </li>



<li>The Examination duration will be 3 hours (180 minutes).</li>



<li>The examination will comprise of 200 MCQ ( Multiple Choice Questions ) inclusive of 100 Knowledge based questions (Marine subjects and 100 English and aptitude-based questions).</li>



<li>Each question answered correctly is awarded 1 mark. There is negative marking of 0.25 for every wrong answer. No marks are awarded or deducted for un-attempted questions.</li>



<li>Testing Areas for knowledge:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Terrestrial Navigation.</li>



<li>Knowledge of navigational instruments.</li>



<li>Navigation- Charts, Lights, shapes and sound signals, concept of time, position plotting fundamentals.</li>



<li>Basic Ship construction and Naval architecture.</li>



<li>Fire Safety- Knowledge of basic firefighting equipment.</li>



<li>Basic cargo work- knowledge of terms.</li>



<li>Basic terms associated with stability, understanding of common terms associated with ships.</li>



<li>Meteorology and Oceanography</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li>Questions in subject knowledge areas will be from approved syllabus of DNS / BSc Nautical Science</li>
</ul>
