<p>Being a Cadet or TME at MSC Shipmanagement Limited is an exciting opportunity for young people who are passionate about a career at sea. Securing an <strong>MSC sponsorship for DNS</strong> is highly sought after. The exam is open to both male and female candidates. After you pass out from the Training Institute,&nbsp;you will be assigned to a company vessel as a deck cadet or trainee marine engineer.</p>



<p>The selection process for the Deck Cadet program consists of three steps:</p>



<ol>
<li>An online entrance exam was conducted by MSC in April 2024.</li>



<li>The Indian Maritime University Common Entrance Test (IMU CET)</li>



<li>A medical examination,&nbsp;psychometric test,&nbsp;and interview.</li>
</ol>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">MSC Exam 2024 Eligibility Criteria:</h2>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">For Deck Cadets Exam Eligibility</h3>



<ul>
<li>Unmarried Indian National</li>



<li>Educational qualifications: 10+2 to comply with 65 marks in pcm aggregate for the dns diploma in nautical science</li>



<li>Candidates awaiting the 12th result can apply</li>



<li>Medical requirements & medical tests:
<ul>
<li>All applicants must&nbsp;comply with the physical and medical standards as per regulations and Company standards.</li>



<li>Medical examination and medical tests will be done&nbsp;by the Company &amp; DG Shipping Approved Doctor ONLY.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li>Age requirements:
<ul>
<li>Minimum age requirement:&nbsp;17 years old as on&nbsp;1st August 2024.</li>



<li>Maximum age limit:&nbsp;19 years old for 10+2 as on&nbsp;1st August 2024.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>Candidates who have completed the B.Sc Nautical Science are NOT Eligible to apply for the DNS program</strong></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">For Engine Cadets Exam Eligibility</h3>



<ul>
<li>Unmarried Indian National</li>



<li>10+2 with 65% marks in PCM aggregate</li>



<li>50% marks in English at 10th or 12th</li>



<li>55% marks in the final year of B.E. or B. Tech. degree</li>



<li>Meet all physical and medical standards as per regulations and Company standards</li>



<li>Minimum age requirement: 17 years old as on August 1, 2024</li>



<li>Maximum age limit: 19 years old as on August 1, 2024</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>Medical Examination will be done by Company &amp; DG Shipping Approved Doctor ONLY</strong>. </p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">MSC Exam 2024 syllabus and pattern:</h2>



<p>The online examination is a 1.5-hour paper with the following, as per the page:</p>



<ul>
<li>English – 15 questions</li>



<li>Maths – 12 questions</li>



<li>Physics – 13 questions</li>



<li>General Knowledge – 10 questions</li>
</ul>



<p>The MSC online examination syllabus includes English, Maths, Physics, and General Knowledge. </p>



<p>All questions are objective-type multiple-choice questions.</p>

<p>IMU Charges – Rs 1000</p>

<p></p>



<p>Upon successful completion of the Deck Cadet program, you will be assigned to a company vessel as a Deck Cadet with a stipend of Rs 27,000 per month. You will gain valuable experience at sea and work under the supervision of qualified officers. After a few years of sea time, you will be eligible to take the exams to become a Third Officer.</p>
