<p><strong>BrightMariner</strong> Started, on 03 March 2021, as an initiative to create the world's largest Maritime Knowledge base that can help seafarers. </p>



<p><strong>Our Aim: A go-to place to search for anything related to the maritime field. </strong></p>



<p>Our Website covers a great collection of study materials and detailed notes in subjects such as Marine Engineering, DNS, IMU-CET, Navigation, Cargo work, Ship operation technology, Meteorology, ROR, Ship Stability, etc. </p>



<p>Since we are at our initial stage and we are just getting started. If anyone wants to contribute to this ecosystem, is welcomed wholeheartedly.</p>



<p>We are a team of Enthusiast mariners who wants to contribute to society by giving back whatever we learned is learning in our life.</p>
